

# HRH Gold Invest — Premium Gold Trading Website

## Overview
A luxury, high-trust business website for HRH Gold Invest, a Ugandan gold company. The site will feature a black, gold, and white color scheme with premium typography (Playfair Display + Inter), smooth animations, and strong calls-to-action throughout.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep black backgrounds, rich gold accents (#D4AF37 / #B8860B), crisp white text
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display for headings (elegant serif), Inter for body text (clean, modern)
- **Style**: Luxury feel with generous spacing, subtle gold gradients, smooth hover effects
- **Layout**: Sticky navbar, clean card-based sections, full-width hero sections

---

## Shared Components
- **Sticky Navbar**: Logo, page links, and "Get a Quote" CTA button with mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer**: Company info, quick links, contact details, and social media placeholders
- **CTA Sections**: Reusable gold-accented call-to-action banners
- **Smooth Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale effects on cards and buttons

## Technical Notes
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- No backend needed — all content is static/frontend-only
- Contact form will show a success toast (no email sending without backend)
